Welcome to Dissenter Preparedness in Japan

This website provides information and resources to help dissenter communities in Japan navigate challenges and advocate for freedom of speech and expression.

Japan has a rich history of social and political activism, and dissenting voices play a crucial role in shaping the country's future. However, it is essential for dissenters to be prepared for the challenges they may face.

This website aims to offer guidance and support to dissenter communities in Japan by providing articles, resources, and information on legal rights, online security, physical safety, and effective communication strategies.
